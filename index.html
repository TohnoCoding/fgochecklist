2024/09/10: Notice of new feature!

I've recently added the ability to hide (and save, both in your local data as well as in in your shortened URL!) any JP-only Servants. I've made sure to test as much as I possibly could to ensure the feature was working 100% correctly; however, I'm human and can make mistakes. If you find anything weird with this, please, I implore you open a new issue on the project repository and write down your findings with as much detail as possible (ideally, with a video recording of your screen; most mobile devices have this capability built-in; if you're on Windows or Mac, you can use Loom, and if you're on Linux, Kooha is a good option).

2024/09/13: Statistics are now fixed for both regular and Class-based mode. Thanks for your patience! Plus, any classes with no Servants in them (like a certain spoilery JP-only class) will not even render if JP-exclusive units are hidden. Enjoy!

~Tohno Shiki
